We are here to restore the lives dementia steals.

We are a team from Ardingly College (UK), united by a mission to deliver the world’s first permanent cure for Alzheimer’s and related dementias through integrated neuromodulation and mitochondrial enhancement.

Our Mission

To pioneer an integrated, non-invasive therapeutic system that permanently reverses cognitive decline by targeting mitochondrial dysfunction—the root cause of neurodegenerative disease. We empower patients and their families with continuous remote care, personalized lifestyle guidance, and direct biological intervention to rebuild the lives they deserve.

Our Vision

A world where Alzheimer’s and dementia are no longer lifelong sentences. We envision a future where aging populations—especially those in underserved rural areas—have access to precise, remote, and curative care, freeing them from the anxiety, confusion, and isolation of neurodegenerative decline.

Who We Serve

Our primary users are aging individuals and their family caregivers, especially those in rural areas where frequent hospital visits are impractical. Through our integrated hardware‑software system, we create a continuous remote network between patients and healthcare professionals—delivering timely medical guidance, symptom relief through mitochondrial enhancement, and peace of mind for families.

With the global population over 65 expanding rapidly, the need for accessible, innovative care has never been greater. We serve both individual subscribers and healthcare institutions, using tiered pricing to ensure wide adoption. The data we collect not only personalizes care but also fuels clinical research, driving ongoing medical progress.

Our Innovation

We operate at the intersection of digital health, non‑invasive neuromodulation, and mitochondrial‑focused therapy. Unlike traditional healthcare—which relies on costly, infrequent visits—or single‑modality devices that lack integration, our solution combines infrared and electrical stimulation to enhance mitochondrial activity, paired with a data‑driven platform for continuous oversight.

This dual approach creates a competitive moat: we provide both biological intervention and clinical connectivity, making us not just a wearable, but a therapeutic ecosystem. While regulatory pathways pose challenges, they also raise barriers to entry—strengthening our position in a large, growing, and profoundly underserved market.

Our Values

  • Cure‑Driven Innovation: We don’t innovate for its own sake. Every breakthrough is directed toward a permanent solution for neurodegenerative disease.
  • Accessible Excellence: Medical‑grade efficacy must reach those who need it most—especially aging populations in remote or underserved communities.
  • Human‑Centric System Design: Our technology is intuitive, compassionate, and built around the daily realities of patients and caregivers.
  • Integrity Through Data: We communicate openly, validate claims with science, and use collected data to advance both personal care and global research.
